Product journey

A clear path through practice

1. Introduction

Orient your attention and settle in with Quote of the Day and brief entry guidance.

2. Foundation

Build baseline skills through Foundation Tracks and consistent chamber reps.

3. Core Stability

Develop steadiness under pressure with focused drills and structured session flow.

4. Applied Awareness

Take practice into real moments and pressure-test awareness beyond the cushion.

5. Reflection

Capture private reflections in your Personal Journal after each training cycle.

6. Insights

Review scoring, recent history, and awareness patterns to choose your Next Best Move.
